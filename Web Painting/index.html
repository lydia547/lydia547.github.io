I'm Zixuan Liu, and you can call me Lydia. I studied art history and films at Trinity college for my first two years of college, and then I transferred to UNC to study communications.

During my college life, I found that I have a marvelous bond with two cities--Shanghai and Tokyo.

I met various people, experienced lots of things, and created my own story here.

SHANGHAI

I studied at Fudan University as a transfer student during my freshman year at Trinity.

I had an internship at UCCA Edge, one of the main modern art museums in Shanghai, and I had another one at MediaBrands, an advertising firm, during the last summer vacation.

TOKYO

Studying several courses about Japanese and Japanese culture at Trinity, I became intrigued by Japan and went on a summer vacation last year to experience it for myself. This summer, I went to Japan again to create a mini documentary that focuses on the accessible facilities in Japan.
