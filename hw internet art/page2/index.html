It is from...

Suzhou embroidery originated in the Wu County area of Suzhou. The Qing Dynasty established “Su, Xiang, Yue, Shu” as China's four famous embroidery. The Qing Dynasty was the flourishing period of Suzhou Embroidery. Su embroidery has a delicate pattern, exquisite conception, meticulous embroidery, lively needlework, elegant colors, and a unique style with strong local characteristics.
