Suzhou Embroidery

Suzhou Embroidery, also known as "Su Embroidery," is one of the oldest and most exquisite embroidery styles in China. With a history of over 2,500 years, it is renowned for its detailed, lifelike, and artistic designs, often featuring flowers, animals, and landscapes. The precision and beauty of Suzhou embroidery have made it an integral part of Chinese cultural heritage.
